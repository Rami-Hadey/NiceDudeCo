Info

The Nice Dude Co Vest is an outer tactical vest used by military and law enforcement agencies at home and abroad due to its efficient design which maximize the operator’s comfort and mobility without sacrificing protection. Our ballistic tactical assault vest is the complete package as a result of its lightweight Kevlar design combined with features such as fully adjustable padded shoulders straps, interior mesh lining for maximum air circulation, and 360 degree coverage for increased load bearing support. Not to mention, this thing deflects the bullet back at the shooter! If you’re looking for a vest that not only protects you, but kills the person shooting at you as well, you’ve come to the right place.

In addition to our basic vest, we offer vest for those who are looking for a more fashionable approach to protection. We partnered with companies ranging from Louis Vuitton to Off-White. We have collabed with multiple companies to fit the more casual and fashionable consumer, you won’t find a more elegant vest anywhere else, Guaranteed.

Lastly, we offer a custom vest. These have all the same features of our basic vest but they allow you to design them to your liking. You can get them custom embroidered with anything you want it to say, such as your name or nickname. These vest will make you stand out among a crowd, Guaranteed!
